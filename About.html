ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Kien. I am a Software Engineering student at FPT University, where I focus on translating complex problems into clean, efficient code. My academic journey has been largely shaped by an enriching Vietnam-Malaysia exchange program, which broadened my global perspective and taught me how to adapt quickly to new environments and challenges.

Alongside my strong foundation in programming, I have a deep, driving passion for graphic design. I believe that the best software doesn't just function flawlessly—it feels intuitive and looks beautiful. Whether I am architecting a backend system or designing the visual flow for a collaborative team presentation, I approach every project with both a developer's precision and a designer's eye.

I thrive in team environments, using my diverse background to bridge the gap between technical constraints and creative possibilities. When I'm not studying or coding, I'm usually experimenting with new design concepts or exploring the intricacies of game mechanics.
